Research on Large Language Models (LLM)

Large Language Models (LLMs) have revolutionized the field of Artificial Intelligence, enabling applications in healthcare, automation, and natural language processing.

Applications of LLMs

  • Healthcare: Predictive analysis, automated diagnostics, and patient assistance using AI-powered chatbots.
  • Education: AI tutors, automated grading, and content summarization.
  • Cybersecurity: Anomaly detection and AI-driven threat assessment.
  • Business Intelligence: Automated report generation and AI-driven insights.

Current Research Topics

  • Enhancing LLMs with Federated Learning for Privacy-Preserving AI.
  • Bias Reduction and Ethical Considerations in AI-driven Decision Making.
  • Optimizing LLMs for Edge Computing Applications.
  • Multimodal AI: Integrating LLMs with Vision and Speech Processing.
